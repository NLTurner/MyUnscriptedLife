In person

We come off the screen, too.

Belonging is hard to feel through a webcam. So the Club gathers in real life on a regular basis, and grows outward from there.

Local gatherings

Casual, regular meetups around the DMV that I host in person. Included with your membership, so you have a standing invitation to a room of people who get it.

Day-events & retreats

Bigger experiences a few times a year that members travel in for. Ticketed on their own, with member pricing and first access before anyone else.

Fit

Who this is really for.

This is for you if

  • You have done everything right and still feel far from yourself
  • You are done performing a life you did not choose
  • You want a method and a community, not another motivational quote
  • You believe it is never too late, even on the days it does not feel that way

This is not for you if

  • You are looking for a quick fix or a five-step hack
  • You want someone to hand you the answers
  • You are not ready to look squarely at where you actually are
  • You want to stay exactly as you are and feel better about it
