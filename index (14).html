The Story

It is never too late to become yourself.

For most of my life, I followed a script. Some of it I was handed by family. Some of it came from work, from expectation, from what a good life was supposed to look like. I was good at following it. I just was not sure how much of it was actually mine.

My Unscripted Life™ is for people in midlife and beyond who have been living by a script they did not write. You have done what you were supposed to do. But the life stopped feeling like yours somewhere along the way, and you are ready to write the next part for yourself.

That is the work we do here: figuring out what is actually yours, and building the next chapter from there. It is never too late to begin.

Nicole L. Turner Founder, My Unscripted Life™

Honestly

This is not for everyone.

This is for you if

  • You have done everything right and still feel far from yourself
  • You are done performing a life you did not choose
  • You want a method and a community, not another motivational quote
  • You believe it is not too late, even on the days it feels that way

This is not for you if

  • You are looking for a quick fix or a five-step hack
  • You want someone to hand you the answers
  • You are not ready to be honest about where you actually are
  • You want to stay exactly as you are and feel better about it
